My Story

From First "Hello World" to Training Transformers

My journey into AI started in high school when I stumbled upon a Python tutorial at 2 AM. That night, I wrote my first script — a simple calculator — and I was hooked. I couldn't believe that a few lines of code could make a machine think.

When I am not training models or debugging CUDA errors, you will find me contributing to open-source projects, writing technical blogs, or mentoring aspiring developers in my university's AI club.

"The goal of AI is not to replace humans, but to amplify human potential in ways we have never imagined."

— My Personal Philosophy
